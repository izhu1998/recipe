Loaded Baked Potato Soup
Reference: Gigi Madanipour. November 8, 2024. https://www.foodnetwork.ca/recipe/loaded-potato-soup/
Prep Time 15 min Cook Time 30 min Yields 6-8 servings
Ingredients:
6 strips bacon
2 Tbsp butter
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
⅓ cup flour
4 cups chicken broth
2 ½ lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 cup cooking cream
¼ tsp red pepper flakes or chili powder optional
¾ cup sour cream
1 cup grated cheddar cheese, plus more for garnish
Salt and pepper to taste
